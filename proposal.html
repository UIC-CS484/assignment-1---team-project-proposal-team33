Music JukeBox

APIs and External Resources:

A list of all the APIs that we might find usefel when seeking for songs and podcasts.

Will use Shazam api to get all the information about songs, albums and artists.

Will use Listen Notes Api to search various Podcasts.

What data will you collect from authenticated site user?

The data fetched from the api will be used to provide the song name, song info, as well as podcast information etc.

Database Schema

USER -> id, firstName, lastName, email, password
SONG -> id, name, artist, lyrics, duration, album, plays
PODCAST -> id, artist, duration, reviews

Potential Users:

The users of this site will be song enthusasist who are looking to explore new
artists and hear new songs.

Motivations:

Our motive is to bring into light the underrated artists and songs that usually go
unnoticed on the popular websites.

We also want to keep fans on track about latest news on his favourite artists and their
upcoming songs.

Goal:

As a team we belive that song is a vital part of everyones life that usually goes unappreciated
and we just want to create this site to all the listners and artists who truly appreciates
music and embrace it in their life.
